Before Registering for State Training

Prerequisites for ALL New York State DHSES/OFPC training are completion of the following FEMA requirements:

  • Obtain a FEMA Student Identification (SID)
  • IS-100.c - Introduction to the Incident Command System
  • IS-700.b - National Incident Management System (NIMS)
